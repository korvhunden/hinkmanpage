Dice

Multiplier Dice

Sum of dice: 0
Score: 0
Turn: 1

How to play:

Enter a value between 3 - 18 and press the button that says "let's roll!"

If your guess equals or is less than the sum of the three top dice it will be multiplied by the multiplier die and added to your score. After 10 rounds the game ends.
